Our Story

Nyandiwa Smart Poultry Connect is more than just a poultry farm. We're a community-driven initiative committed to providing fresh, quality eggs while supporting local agriculture.

Our Mission

To revolutionize poultry farming in Siaya County through smart technology and sustainable practices, while providing fresh, high-quality eggs to our community at fair prices.

Our Vision

To become the leading smart poultry farm in Kenya, setting industry standards for quality, efficiency, and customer service while promoting sustainable agricultural practices.

Our Journey

2020

Foundation

Started as a small family-owned poultry farm with just 100 chickens in Siaya County.

2021

Expansion

Expanded operations to 500 chickens and introduced modern poultry management systems.

2022

Technology Integration

Implemented smart monitoring systems and launched our online ordering platform.

2023

Community Impact

Established partnerships with local businesses and started our community outreach programs.
