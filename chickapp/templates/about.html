OUR MISSION

Committed to Fresh Quality & Community Value

Nyandiwa Smart Poultry is a community-driven initiative dedicated to providing fresh, high-quality eggs while supporting local agriculture and sustainable farming practices.

Community Focused
Sustainable Farming

Our Core Values

Our Mission

To revolutionize poultry farming in Siaya County through innovative technology and sustainable practices, while providing fresh, high-quality eggs to our community at fair and competitive prices.

Our Vision

To become the leading smart poultry farm in Kenya, setting industry standards for quality, efficiency, and excellent customer service while championing sustainable agricultural practices.

Why Choose Nyandiwa Smart Poultry?

100% Fresh & Quality

All our eggs are collected daily and delivered fresh. We maintain strict quality standards to ensure the best for our customers.

Sustainable Farming

We use eco-friendly practices and ensure our hens are treated humanely in a clean, healthy environment.

Fast & Reliable

Order online and get your eggs delivered quickly. We offer multiple delivery options with flexible scheduling.

Our Journey & Growth

2020

Foundation

Started as a small family farm with 100 chickens in Siaya County.

2021

Expansion

Grew to 500 chickens and introduced smart poultry management systems.

2022

Digital Innovation

Launched online ordering platform and smart delivery tracking system.

2024

Community Leadership

Became a trusted local brand with community partnerships & outreach.

Our Growth Story

From a small family farm, we've grown into a trusted supplier serving hundreds of customers across Siaya County with fresh eggs and exceptional service.
